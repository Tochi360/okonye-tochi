    Primary Health Care

  • Problem:

    Inaccessibility of primary health care in Nigeria

Solution

Thought Process

There are numerous problems facing the primary health Institution in Nigeria, they include Lack of equipment, Lack of qualified Personnel, Illiteracy, Funding, Corruption e.t.c. While the solution to most of the problem require government intervention, some can be solved with good and inclusive User-centered- Design.
This app would help people from both rural and urban area access Health better and also educate them about some basic health and First aid Tips.
The features includes,
Find clinic feature to help people find government approval health center close to them.
Book appointment feature, helps you appointment with your Physician and helps you keep tab and your next appointment.
Chat with qualified personnel feature, a chat session between patients and qualified registered health personnel.
Blood Donor feature, connect people who are in need of blood donation to government approved blood bank and people within their close proximity willing to donor blood.
Daily health tips feature.
Yeah I know they sound bogus, but I believe that Primary health issues can be solved with proper communication of all the Stakeholders i.e the Government, Community and Health personnel, and that is what this product is aimed at achieving COMMUNICATION.
N.B. The content of this products will be written in English Hausa Yoruba and Igbo, for more accessibility.
