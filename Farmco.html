This product is a B2B Agro e-commerce app aimed at connect Farmers with clients and also Farmers with raw materials distributors.
Features would include,
Marketplace a place to buy and sell agro product.
Dashboard to help the farmer keep Inventory of all products sold and the ones in queue.
A chat session between Buyers and Farmers.
N.B. The content of this products will be written in English Hausa Yoruba and Igbo, for more accessibility.
